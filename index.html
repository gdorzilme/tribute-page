Here's a timeline of Nelson Mandela's life:

  • 1918 - Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela was born on July 18th in Mvezo, Cape Province, Union of South Africa
  • 1938 - Mandela goes to University of Fort Hare, but is expelled 2 years later for participating in a student strike.
  • 1944 - Mandela marries Evelyn Ntoko Mase. The couple would go on to have four kids, but the marriage falls apart and they divorce in 1958.
  • 1948 - The National Party comes into power in South Africa setting the stage for apartheid
  • 1956 - Mandela is arrested on December 6 and charged with high treason
  • 1958 - While battling the charges of high treason, Mandela marries his second wife, Winnie Nomzamo Madikizela, in June of that year
  • 1960 - On March 21, police open fire on a demonstration and kill 69 black protesters in Sharpeville.
  • 1962 - Mandela is arrested on August 5 after returning to South Africa from a trip abroad.
  • 1964 - Mandela and seven others are sentenced to life imprisonment.
  • 1980 - An international “Free Mandela” campaign ends with the call for his release by the UN Security Council
  • 1985 - On January 31, South Africa’s president P.W. Botha offers to pardon Mandela if he renounces the violence. Mandela refuses and insists that the government ends apartheid and grants political rights to black
  • 1989 - Mandela receives is bachelor of laws degree on May 17 via correspondence with the University of South Africa
  • 1990 - Feb 11 Mandela is fred after 27 years in prison
  • 1994 - South African apartheid formally ends on April 27 when the ANC wins a majority of the vote and Mandela is elected president
  • 1994 - On May 10, Mandela is inaugurated as president
  • 1996 - He divorces Winnie Mandela on March 19
  • 1998 - Mandela marries for third time at 80 yrs old (July 18)
  • 2004 - Retirement from public life
  • 2013 - He is hospitalized with lung infection on June 8
  • 2013 - On December 5, Mandela dies peacefully at the age of 95 surrounded by family
